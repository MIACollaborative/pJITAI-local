How is a pJITAI different from a JITAI?

An implicit rationale underlying a JITAI is that the decision rules, once constructed, are fixed and need not be altered. That is, the way in which the tailoring variables are used by the decision rule need not be altered to ensure the optimal effectiveness of the intervention component for new users.
