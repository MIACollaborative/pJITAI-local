  • Explanation

Intervention components are the means of your intervention. In this current version of pJITAI, we ask you to design one intervention component at a time. If you want to incorporate personalization in multiple intervention components in your study, you design a personalizing JITAI for each component.

For example, in the HeartSteps study, the intervention components are: 1) activity suggestion and 2) planning for the next day’s activities. In the HeartSteps demo, we demonstrate designing the “activity suggestion” intervention component.

We suggest that if you are not familiar with our toolbox, you start by going through our HeartSteps demo (which can be created by clicking "Add New Project" and "Add HeartSteps Demo" in the left menu). If you cannot see a left menu with an option to "Add New Project," you may see a menu-like icon on the top right corner of your screen. Click it to open the left menu. When you’re ready to use this tool to develop personalizing JITAIs for your study, click "Add New Project" and "Add a new intervention component."
